1. In **Source Flow**, drag a **Modbus Read** node.
2. Double-click the node, and then click <img src="http://communityimage2.oss-cn-hangzhou.aliyuncs.com/84.png" /> next to **Server** to enter the information of your modbus server.

<img width={300} src="http://communityimage2.oss-cn-hangzhou.aliyuncs.com/89.png" />

3. CLick **Add**, and then enter the information of the data to be connected.

<img width={300} src="http://communityimage2.oss-cn-hangzhou.aliyuncs.com/90.png" />

4. Click **Done**.
5. Connect a debug node at the end.
6. Click **Deploy** at the upper-right corner, and then click **Modbus Read** node to trigger the flow.
