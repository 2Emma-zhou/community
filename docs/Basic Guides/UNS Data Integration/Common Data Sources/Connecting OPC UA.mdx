1. In **SourceFlow**, drag an **inject** node.
2. Double-click the node, and then enter the **NodeId** from your OPC UA server.

<img width={300} src="http://communityimage2.oss-cn-hangzhou.aliyuncs.com/83.png" />

3. Drag an **OpcUa-Client** node, and then double-click it.
4. Click <img src="http://communityimage2.oss-cn-hangzhou.aliyuncs.com/84.png" /> next to **Endpoint** to enter the information of your OPC UA server.

<img width={300} src="http://communityimage2.oss-cn-hangzhou.aliyuncs.com/85.png" />

5. Click **Add**, and then set the **Action** to **READ**.
6. Leave everything else as default, and then click **Done**.

<img width={300} src="http://communityimage2.oss-cn-hangzhou.aliyuncs.com/86.png" />

7. Connect the **inject** node to the **OpcUa-Client** node, and then connect a debug node at the end.
8. Click **Deploy** at the upper-right corner, and then click **inject** node to trigger the flow.

<img width={600} src="http://communityimage2.oss-cn-hangzhou.aliyuncs.com/87.png" />
