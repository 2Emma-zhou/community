Multiple languages are supported in supOS to meet the needs of users from different regions.

## Editing Existing I18n
1. Log in to supOS, and then select **System** > **I18n Management**.
2. Select a module from the left side.

<img src="http://communityimage2.oss-cn-hangzhou.aliyuncs.com/158.png" />

<table>
        <thead>
            <tr>
                <th>No.</th>
                <th>Name</th>
                <th>Description</th>
            </tr>
        </thead>
        <tbody>
            <tr>
                <td>1</td>
                <td>Module</td>
                <td>Lists modules whose i18n can be modified.</td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
                <td>2</td>
                <td>Entry</td>
                <td>Lists all i18n entries of the selected module. Adding, editing and deleting entries are available.</td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
                <td>3</td>
                <td>Locales Settings</td>
                <td>Download existing language packs and upload new packs as needed.</td>
            </tr>
        </tbody>
    </table>

3. Browse the entry list, select an entry and click **Edit** under **Operation** to start editing.

<img width={350} src="http://communityimage2.oss-cn-hangzhou.aliyuncs.com/160.png" />

4. Click **Save**.
:::tip
Directly click on the translation box to edit the content of an entry, and then click on the page, the change is automatically saved.
:::

## Adding I18n Entry
1. Click **New Entry** at the upper-right corner.
2. Enter the entry key and the corresponding translations.

<img width={350} src="http://communityimage2.oss-cn-hangzhou.aliyuncs.com/159.png" />

3. Click **Save**.

## Managing Language Packs
1. Click **Locales Settings** at the upper-right corner.
2. Select **New Language** on the left side, and then click **Download Template**.

<img width={350} src="http://communityimage2.oss-cn-hangzhou.aliyuncs.com/161.png" />

3. Fill in the template, and then click <img width={20} src="http://communityimage2.oss-cn-hangzhou.aliyuncs.com/162.png" /> on the same window to upload the file.

<img width={350} src="http://communityimage2.oss-cn-hangzhou.aliyuncs.com/161.png" />

:::tip how to fill in the template
1. Select an eixsting language on the left side, and then click <img src="http://communityimage2.oss-cn-hangzhou.aliyuncs.com/163.png" /> to download the pack.
2. Hover over **Exported** on the top of the window, and then select the language file to download.
3. Edit the downloaded file, and then upload it as a new language pack.
:::
